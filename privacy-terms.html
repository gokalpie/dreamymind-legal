1. Information We Collect

DreamyMind ("we," "our," or "us") collects the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile photo (when you sign in with Google or Apple)
  • Dream Content: Dreams you record, interpretations, tags, and related metadata
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage patterns, and analytics data
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version, and unique identifiers

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Crash reports and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Analytics data to improve app functionality (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising identifiers for personalized ads (if applicable)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: Storing and managing your dream journal entries
  • AI Interpretation: Processing dream content through OpenAI's services to provide interpretations
  • Personalization: Customizing your app experience and recommendations
  • Communication: Sending important updates, notifications, and customer support
  • Analytics: Understanding app usage to improve features and performance
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and protecting our rights

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • OpenAI: Dream content is sent to OpenAI's API for interpretation. To provide this service and enhance AI capabilities, we may allow OpenAI to use anonymized dream data to improve their services and models. This helps us maintain affordable interpretation services. All shared data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  • Firebase/Google: For authentication, database storage, analytics, and crash reporting
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple/Google: For app store transactions and services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (located in the United States)
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Strict access controls limit who can view your data
  • Data Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed for legal purposes

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data. Please note that complete deletion from our backup systems may take up to 30 days
  • Portability: Export your dream data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and advertising tracking

6. Children's Privacy

DreamyMind is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: AI interpretation services (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Firebase: Backend services (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (Privacy Policy)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
